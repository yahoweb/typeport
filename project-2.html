Project_002 : Hybrid Publication

For this project, I selected the Korean film Oldboy and translated its gothic intensity and striking color language into a hybrid publication. I produced a script-based book that conveys the film’s atmosphere through controlled layout, imagery, and color, supported by a package inspired by key film props. The accompanying website extends the experience by presenting music, stills, and a concise synopsis through a cassette-tape–based concept.
